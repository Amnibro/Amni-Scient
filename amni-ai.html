HOW IT WORKS

1. ATLAS LOOKUP

Your query enters the Dictionary Atlas. Reffelt Constant Nonces resolve words to deterministic 512D vectors. The Fractal Palace runs GPU cosine similarity across the full atlas with Hilbert-curve routing and 5-channel MIP texture pages. Domain classified, context extracted, facts gathered — all in a single GPU pass.

2. DUAL-MIND DEBATE

The 2B Scout proposes a response from atlas context. The 9B Deliberator audits it across factual accuracy, safety compliance, response quality, and hallucination risk. If they disagree, consensus arbitration runs. Roles rotate every 10 generations. The Asimov layer screens every output before delivery.

3. SELF-EVOLUTION

Nine growth modes run continuously via Bayesian Thompson Sampling. Weakness-fill patches gaps. Truth-challenge questions assumptions. Void-tether explores speculative hypotheses with confidence decay and collapse detection. Self-optimize analyzes its own bottlenecks. All verified facts commit to the learning sandbox automatically.

4. KNOWLEDGE SHARING

Verified learnings export as .amnipak texture packs. The KnowledgeNet relay accepts community contributions — each pack is Asimov-screened, PII-scrubbed, and SHA-256 integrity checked. Auto-sync pulls new packs nightly. Your AI grows from the community; the community grows from you.

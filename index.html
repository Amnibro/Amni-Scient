WHAT WE BUILD & WHY

Every Amni-Scient project exists because existing solutions fell short in privacy, transparency, or accessibility. Rather than accept those tradeoffs, we built alternatives from scratch.

Web browsers ship with telemetry baked in, ad blocking as an afterthought, and sync services that hold your data hostage. Amni-Browse is a single Rust binary with zero telemetry, navigation-layer ad blocking, DNS-over-HTTPS, and an AES-256-GCM password vault — everything local, nothing phoned home.

Engineering calculators are usually either expensive desktop suites or imprecise web forms. Amni-Calc runs FEA beam analysis, seal design, bolt torque, and thermal calculations in a WASM backend — interactive, accurate, and free.

Educational platforms for children commonly track behavior, serve ads, or require accounts. Amni-Learn collects zero data, runs offline after loading, and covers Pre-K through high school with 60+ interactive games.

WHAT WE BUILD & WHY

Every Amni-Scient project exists because existing solutions fell short in privacy, transparency, or accessibility. Rather than accept those tradeoffs, we built alternatives from scratch.

File encryption apps typically hide their algorithms behind vague marketing. Amni-Crypt publishes its full cipher specification — block size, round count, key derivation parameters, shadow protocol mechanics — because real security comes from mathematical strength, not obscurity.

Space exploration tools tend to be either oversimplified or require powerful gaming hardware. Amni-Explore renders an 80,000-particle galaxy with real NASA exoplanet data using a 97 KB WASM binary that runs on any WebGL2 browser.

Educational platforms for children commonly track behavior, serve ads, or require accounts. Amni-Learn collects zero data, runs offline after loading, and covers Pre-K through high school with 12+ interactive games.

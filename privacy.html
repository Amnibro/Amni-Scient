PRIVACY POLICIES

PER-PRODUCT DATA HANDLING

Each product has different data requirements. Each gets its own policy. No blanket statements — just honest, specific disclosures.

ANDROID

AMNI-CRYPT

File encryption. All cryptographic operations local. No accounts. Free tier ad-supported (AdMob collects device/ad data per Google's policy). Premium removes ads entirely.

 ANDROID

AMNI-HAVEN

Mobile client for ANCSemi's Haven servers. Data transmitted to your chosen server. Free tier ad-supported (AdMob). FCM for push notifications. Server-side data governed by your operator.

 DESKTOP

AMNI-AI

Local intelligence engine. Fully offline. No accounts, no cloud, no telemetry. Everything runs on your hardware.

 PLATFORM

AMNI-CORE

Systems platform. No telemetry, no data collection, no phone-home. Operates entirely on local hardware.

 WEB APP

AMNI-EXPLORE

Browser-based galaxy explorer. All rendering runs locally via WebGL/WASM. No accounts, no storage. AdSense on hosting page per Google's policy.

NAVIGATE THE WORD

Imago Nuntii Divini renders the entire Douay-Rheims Catholic Bible as a live 3D pointcloud. Every verse is a node; every cross-reference is an edge. Click any verse to fly to it, read its chapter, and trace its connections through scripture. Tandem Mode splits the screen into book reading + visualizer + connected passages.

Open directly in your browser — no download, no account, no install required. Full-screen interactive 3D pointcloud.

HOW IT WORKS

✍

DOUAY-RHEIMS TEXT

Full Catholic canon including Deuterocanonical books (Tobit, Judith, 1–2 Maccabees, Wisdom, Sirach, Baruch). Complete verse text accessible via double-click on any node.

⚙

RUST / WASM ENGINE

The humanoid Silhouette layout is computed by a compiled Rust binary (97 KB .wasm) for maximum performance and IP protection.

🌐

THREE.JS RENDERER

Custom ShaderMaterial with per-point sizing, pulse animation on selected nodes, additive-blended edges, and smooth 60-frame camera fly-to on selection.

🔭

CROSS-REFERENCE GRAPH

64,497 edges connect related verses across the entire canon. Select any verse and its connections glow gold — tracing theological threads through both Testaments.
